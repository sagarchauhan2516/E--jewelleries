Ring Size Guide

1. Find a piece of string or strip of paper.
2. Wrap it snugly around the base of your finger.
3. Mark where the ends meet.
4. Measure the length with a ruler to find your circumference.
5. Compare it with the chart below to find your ring size.

Bangle Size Guide

Measure across the widest part of your hand (without squeezing) to find your bangle size:

How to Measure Your Bangle Size

Follow these simple steps to find your perfect bangle size:

Step 1: Measure Your Hand

Take a flexible measuring tape or a strip of paper and wrap it around the widest part of your hand, usually around the knuckles. Make sure the tape is snug but not tight.

Step 2: Measure the Diameter of an Existing Bangle

If you already have a bangle that fits well, place it on a flat surface and use a ruler to measure the inside diameter of the bangle in millimeters (mm).

Hand Width (mm) Bangle Size (Indian)

63.5 mm2-6

Tips:

  • Compare your hand circumference and inner diameter measurement to a size chart.
  • Remember, 1 cm = 10 mm. Accuracy is key for comfort!
  • When in doubt, choose the larger size for a more comfortable fit.

Bracelet Size Guide

Measure your wrist with a flexible tape measure and add 1-2 cm for comfort:

How to Measure Your Bracelet Size

Follow these simple steps to find your correct bracelet size:

Step 1: Wrap a Strip Around Your Wrist

Take a flexible strip of paper, string, or a soft measuring tape and wrap it snugly around your wrist where you would normally wear a bracelet.

Step 2: Mark the Point

Use a pen or marker to note the exact point where the strip overlaps or meets around your wrist.

Step 3: Measure the Length

Lay the strip flat against a ruler and measure the length in centimeters or inches to find your wrist circumference. This is your bracelet size.

Wrist Circumference (cm) Bracelet Size
14 - 15 cmSmall
16 - 17 cmMedium
18 - 19 cmLarge
20 - 21 cmExtra Large

Tips:

  • For a snug fit, add 0.5 cm (or ¼ inch) to your wrist size.
  • For a looser, more comfortable fit, add 1-2 cm (or about ¾ inch).
  • Measure your wrist at the end of the day when it’s slightly larger for the best accuracy.

Necklace Size Guide

Choose the perfect necklace length depending on your preference:

How to Choose the Right Necklace Length

Choosing the right necklace length enhances the beauty of your neckline and matches your outfit perfectly. Here's a visual guide to standard necklace lengths and how they typically sit on your body:

Standard Necklace Lengths:

  • 30 cm / 12" – Collar: Fits snugly around the neck.
  • 35 cm / 14" – Choker: Rests closely against the base of the neck.
  • 40 cm / 16" – Princess: Falls just below the throat at the collarbone.
  • 45 cm / 18" – Princess (Standard): Hangs over the collarbone.
  • 50 cm / 20" – Matinee: Falls a few inches below the collarbone.
  • 55 cm / 22" – Matinee: Reaches the top of the bust.
  • 60 cm / 24" – Opera: Falls slightly below the bust line.
  • 70 cm / 28" – Opera: Lower bust length, elegant drop.
  • 80 cm / 31" – Rope: Can be doubled over for a layered look.

Tips for Choosing the Right Necklace:

  • Consider the neckline of your outfit — shorter necklaces pair well with higher necklines, and longer necklaces complement lower ones.
  • Layer multiple chains of different lengths for a trendy look!
  • When in doubt, 45 cm (18") is the most common, universally flattering size.
